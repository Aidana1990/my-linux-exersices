Step 4: Save and quit.

Step 5 : Type less system_log.txt. Once the log file opens in less, you'll see the system events and messages displayed.

Step 6 : Press ' Ctrl+F ' or ' Space ' to move forward one window, Press' Ctrl+B ' to move backwards.

Step 7 : To jump to the beginning of the file, press lowercase g, and to jump to the end, press uppercase G.

Step 8 : Type n to go to the next occurrence of a searched term, or uppercase N to go to the previous occurrence.

Step 9: Let's search for the term "error" within the log file. Type /ERROR and press Enter. The system will highlight occurrences of "ERROR" in the file.

Step 10 : To search backward for a term, for example, "WARNING," type ?WARNING and press Enter.

Step 11: Use the navigation keys to move forward or backward and observe the highlighted occurrences of the searched term.

Step 12 : When you're finished exploring, press q to quit less and return to the command prompt.

Step 13: Now, to complete your quest, it's time to uncover the elusive "WARNING" messages within the log file. Use your newfound knowledge to summon the command less [filename] | grep -i warning. With this incantation, the warnings shall reveal themselves, and your quest shall be complete!
